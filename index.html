2017 Car Data by Fuel Types

This webpage shows AverageCityMPG vs AverageHighwayMPG for cars based on three different fuel types. The graphs also shed light on a car's fuel efficiency via the city and highway MPG.
Click on each of the fuel type buttons below to see the cars that have that specific fuel type. Clicking on "All" shows cars of all the three fuel types.
Additionally, you can hover over points to view a car's Make, AverageCityMPG, AverageHighwayMPG, and Number of Cylinders.
