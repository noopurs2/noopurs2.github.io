Car Fuel Efficieny by Fuel Types

This webpage shows the AverageCityMPG vs AverageHighwayMPG for cars based on three different fuel types based on car data collected in 2017. The graphs also shed light on a car's fuel efficiency via the Average City and Highway MPG.
Click on each of the fuel type buttons below to see the cars that have that specific fuel type. Click on "All" to see cars of all three fuel types.
Additionally, you can hover over the data points to view a car's Make, AverageCityMPG, AverageHighwayMPG, and Number of Cylinders.
